Common Church Website Challenges

Many church sites become fragile over time—usually because responsibility gets unclear and maintenance becomes ad-hoc.

Outdated, but risky to touch

Changes feel dangerous because the site is brittle or undocumented.

No clear responsibility

No one knows who is responsible for decisions and follow-through.

Volunteer dependency

A stretched volunteer is the only person who knows how it works.

Security & reliability concerns

Updates, backups, privacy, and uptime feel uncertain.

Slow, confusing updates

Simple requests become expensive, delayed, or hard to explain.

Support is inconsistent

Timely help is unreliable, and you often have to re-explain context to new people.
